Virtual Reality and Mixed Reality

Introduction to Virtual Reality (VR)

Virtual Reality (VR) is a groundbreaking technology that immerses users in a fully digital, computer-generated environment. By wearing a VR headset and sometimes additional accessories like hand controllers or haptic gloves, individuals can experience a simulated world that feels as though it exists in three dimensions. This interaction is achieved through the combination of visual, auditory, and sensory input, offering a feeling of presence and immersion that makes users feel as if they are physically present in the virtual environment.
